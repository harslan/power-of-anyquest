What is AnyQuest?

A collaborative AI platform for enterprises and universities.

Core building blocks

  • Workspaces: people, settings, and context
  • Content repository: docs + datasets (RAG)
  • Assistants: private dialog, attached files
  • Agents: no‑code taskflows (Generate · Retrieve · Merge · Store · Invoke)
  • Governance: roles, privacy, policy control

Why it matters for faculty & leadership

  • Consistent outputs (templates, prompts)
  • Reusable assets (teaching packs, memos)
  • Faster prep & research synthesis
  • Strategic insights for the Dean’s office
